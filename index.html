How it works?

You can integrate Route Optimization API in your product in three simple steps

1. Get your API key for your app

2. Define your operations by creating an Organization, it's delivery Tasks and Drivers responsible for delivery

3. Optimize Route to assign tasks to drivers based on optimum route algorithm for efficiently completing your delivery tasks
