About

We at AlternateParts are a team of Engineers and Computer Scientists trying to provide state of the art Optimization tools. At the moment, via APIs.

The companies that offer Optimization Softwares, quite ironically, make these tools available for Suboptimal price and performance.

Our Products

At the moment, Route Optimization APIs is our main service. Sometimes we also publish some non-commercial experimental tools for engineers and hackers to experiment with.

Route Optimation (RO) APIs

AlternateParts - RO APIs are very easy to integrate APIs for developers and startups to integrate Route Optimization feature into your products.

The problem with existing Route Optimization products and APIs is that they are really expensive and they push to sell their complete platform to you. As a food delivery business just starting, you definitely do not want to deal with a product to manage fleets of large vehicles.

At some point when your startup grows, this cost of a 3rd party service becomes a big cost and bottleneck. So, almost every company tries to build an inhouse alternate for these 3rd party optimization services. We know because that is what we tried to do when we were working on a pickup and delivery startup.

With RO APIs, you integrate Route Optimization into your softwares and apps as if they are part of your tech stack. Since you do not worry about implementing a state of the art Route Optimization algorithm, neither do you have to worry about the exponentially increasing costs of other RO tools.

Stock Cuts Planner

Stock Cuts Planner is an experimental project that we did over a few weekends. It helps in planning in advance how to cut Paper Rolls, Metal Rods and Sheets, Wood and Glass Sheets so as to minimize the total stock used and waste incurred.

In academic literature, this problem is known as the Cutting Stock Problem.
